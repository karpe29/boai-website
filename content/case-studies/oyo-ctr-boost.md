**OYO Boosted CTR by up to 186% with BlinkofAI**

OYO Rooms, one of India's largest hospitality brands, runs frequent digital campaigns.

---

## Challenge

The primary goal was to scale performance marketing campaigns using video ads that were:

* **On-brand**
* **Engaging**
* **Personalized**

## Solution

BlinkofAI provided on-brand video ads personalized to specific personas and platforms. This approach included:

* **Variations**: Customization by duration, language, messaging, and aspect ratios.
* **Brand Fidelity**: Consistent brand identity maintained through AI-powered asset preparation combined with human-in-the-loop review.

---

## Performance Results

The implementation led to a significant CTR uplift across various campaigns, ranging from **35% to 186%**.

### Campaign Comparison Table

| Campaign Type | CTR before BlinkofAI | CTR with BlinkofAI | Uplift |
|--------------|---------------------|-------------------|--------|
| Long Weekend Promo | 0.53% | 0.90% | 70% |
| Brand Video (Awareness) | 0.60% | 0.89% | 48% |
| Price-Focused Campaign | 0.49% | 1.40% | 186% |
| Category Promo (UMC) | ~0.54% | 1.05% | +94% est. |
| Seasonal Campaign (Vtine) | ~0.54% | 0.73% | +35% est. |

**Summary Metrics:**

* **Pre-BlinkofAI CTR average:** ~0.54%
* **Post-BlinkofAI CTR range:** 0.73% to 1.40%

---

## Conclusion

BlinkofAI helped OYO break through traditional content bottlenecks. This collaboration achieved measurable improvements in CTR, driving significantly better Marketing ROI.
