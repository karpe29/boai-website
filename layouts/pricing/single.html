FAQs

Q: What's included in my monthly subscription?

A: Each plan includes a set number of Master Video Ads and Variants per month. Unused videos and variants roll over to the next month while your subscription is active.

Q: Can I use pay-as-you-go pricing with a subscription?

A: Yes! If you need more videos than your plan includes, you can purchase additional videos at $120 (Basic) or $200 (Advanced) per video

Q: What if I go over my plan limits?

A: You'll be notified when approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional videos on-demand.

Q: Can I get custom video done?

A: Yes! For custom requirements beyond our plans (special formats, rush delivery, white-label), email us at hello@blinkofai.io or book a call. We'll create a custom package for your needs.
