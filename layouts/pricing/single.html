We're here to answer all your questions.

Can BlinkofAI's output be trusted to take actions?

Absolutely. We have put all the checks in place to make sure you only get accurate information. BlinkofAI is being reliably used by some of the largest performance marketing teams across the world for ad accounts with 7-8 figure spends. That said, it is early days for LLMs and if you do notice an error, please bring it to us.

How can I start using BlinkofAI?

We recommend scheduling a 30-min introductory chat with the founders as the first step. Every growth team needs a different set of tools and setup depending on your scale, speed of growth, and constraints. So we prefer to onboard each customer by first understanding the requirements and then sharing access to the platform.

Is it safe to connect my ad accounts to BlinkofAI?

We understand that privacy is an utmost concern when you're handling confidential ad performance data. Rest assured, BlinkofAI is SOC-2 compliant, uses only trusted LLM providers and never stores any of your data without permission. You can opt to remove your account at any time.

How does pricing work?

Our Starter plan starts at $999/month per integration, billed annually. For Professional and Enterprise plans, pricing is custom and flexes with your team's scale and scope. We realize that each team is unique so we prefer to custom design a plan that works for you and your team.
